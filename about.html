About Me!

Hello, I'm Stephanie and this is my sweet pup Fern

Welcome to my page! I am a recently "retired" real estate agent with over a decade of experience. I have a strong interest in environmental issues and justice. Born and raised in Pierce County and a 2005 Kitsap County transplant, I love everything the PNW offers and my primary goal every week is to get to the Olympics. Otherwise I enjoy engaging in activities like telling my dog how cute she is, eating the amazing food my spouse cooks, supporting women in sports, and gardening.

I am a proud parent to three daughters and a grandmother to a lively toddler. Currently, I am completing my Environmental Science degree, which I began over 15 years ago.

Dedicated to service, I have volunteered with various organizations, including search and rescue and as an Ombudsman during my time as a military spouse. I am currently involved in my community by updating our neighborhood website and putting on events like the Manette Edible Garden Tour.

I created this space to share my passions, have fun, and exchange impactful stories and ideas with you. Feel free to explore and connect!
